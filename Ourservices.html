Technology Upgrade Notice

During a technology assessment or project implementation, Autonomous IT evaluates the reliability, security, and performance of existing equipment such as routers, firewalls, switches, and Wi-Fi systems.

In some cases, older or consumer-grade equipment may not meet modern security standards or performance requirements. When this occurs, recommended upgrades may be necessary to ensure the network operates securely, reliably, and efficiently.

Our goal is always to make the best use of existing infrastructure when possible. However, when technology limitations create security risks or performance issues, we provide clear recommendations for modern solutions designed to support long-term stability and protection.
