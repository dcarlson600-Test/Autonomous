About Autonomous IT
Autonomous IT was built on a simple idea — technology should work reliably, securely, and efficiently without constant frustration.
Autonomous IT was founded by a cybersecurity professional with nearly a decade of experience protecting systems and teaching security principles. For over 8 years, I have worked in cybersecurity helping organizations strengthen their defenses, improve their infrastructure, and reduce technology risk. For the past 5 years, I have also taught cybersecurity, helping students understand how modern threats work and how systems can be designed securely. Through this experience, I saw that many small businesses struggle with technology that is unreliable, insecure, or overly complicated. Autonomous IT was created to change that. Our goal is to design secure, modern technology environments that are simple, reliable, and built to support the people who depend on them.
