About Autonomous IT
Autonomous IT was built on a simple belief: technology should work reliably, securely, and efficiently for both small businesses and modern homes. We design and support systems that run smoothly in the background, protecting your data, improving performance, and giving you the confidence that your technology is working exactly as it should.
Autonomous IT was founded by a cybersecurity professional with nearly a decade of experience protecting systems and teaching security principles. For over 8 years, I have worked in cybersecurity helping organizations strengthen their defenses, improve their infrastructure, and reduce technology risk. For the past 5 years, I have also taught cybersecurity, helping students understand how modern threats work and how systems can be designed securely. Through this experience, I saw that many small businesses struggle with technology that is unreliable, insecure, or overly complicated. Autonomous IT was created to change that. Our goal is to design secure, modern technology environments that are simple, reliable, and built to support the people who depend on them.

Most small businesses rely on technology every day, but many don’t have a dedicated IT team. In fact, about 52% of small businesses manage their technology themselves or rely on employees who already have other responsibilities. At the same time, 43% of cyberattacks target small businesses, and many organizations are not fully prepared to defend against them. Even short technology outages can disrupt operations, cost productivity, and create unnecessary stress. Because of this, many businesses choose to work with a trusted IT partner. In fact, more than one-third of small businesses outsource IT support to keep their systems secure and running reliably. Autonomous IT helps small businesses and modern homes keep their technology secure, reliable, and running smoothly. Instead of waiting for problems to happen, we focus on building strong systems, improving security, and automating the parts of technology that often cause frustration. The goal is simple: technology that works quietly in the background so you can focus on running your business. Secure. Reliable. Efficient.
